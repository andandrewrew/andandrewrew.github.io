This Light Combo
December 10-20 2019
Combo Milano - Milan, Italy

This Light was invited to develop cultural programming for Combo Milano, a hotel in Milan's Navigli district. Focusing on a large flat panel monitor in the hotel's laundry room lounge, This Light programmed daily 18 hour screenings oriented around place, labor, and leisure. Film menus were distributed to guests every morning with fresh bedding and towels.
